About Me

I'm Joel Tito, a 20-year-old Computer Science & Engineering (IoT) student at CHRIST University, Bengaluru. I specialize in IoT systems, cybersecurity, networking, and cloud computing with a strong blue-team defensive mindset.

My expertise spans Python, C, Java, Rust, Django REST APIs, MongoDB, and embedded systems (ESP32-S3, Arduino). I focus on secure system design, monitoring, and reliability rather than offensive exploitation.

Beyond coding, I'm a competitive programmer, hackathon finalist, university cricket player, and long-distance cyclist. I believe in building robust, secure systems through hands-on engineering and continuous learning.
